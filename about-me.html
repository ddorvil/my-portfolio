About Me

Hi its me, Dominique. Feel free to call me Dom. It's nice to meet you!

I was born and raised in Brooklyn, New York and now live in Rochester attending the University of Rochester.

I am currently a fourth year student and double majoring in Computer Science and Digital Media Studies. I have a specific interest in user experience, graphic design, digital art and web/app development.

In my free time you can find me painting, posing for the 'gram', binge-watching shows, and writing my second book. FUN FACT: I published my first book at 11 years old!
